About the Bride

Jonah P. Carmelotes

Meet Jonah P. Carmelotes, the youngest among her siblings — a 26-year-old woman currently working at Virginia Food Inc. A kind-hearted and generous person, Jonah always finds ways to share her blessings with others whenever she can.

She loves the colors blue and white, finds peace in both the mountains and the sea, and enjoys listening to worship songs and reading books during her quiet moments. She also has a soft spot for Jollibee and coffee.

Growing up, Jonah witnessed the hardships of life at an early age. Those experiences shaped her into the strong, determined, and ambitious woman she is today. Now, at the age of 26, she is finally marrying the man she once prayed for — the one her heart has waited for, loved, and chosen through faith and time.

About the Groom

John Paul A. Martinez

Meet John Paul A. Martinez, born on June 10, 2000 — now 25 years old. He is the youngest in their family and a man known for his quiet strength and humble personality.

At a young age, John Paul had to stop going to school and choose to work instead so he could help support his family. Life may not have been easy for him, but those experiences shaped him into the hardworking and responsible man he is today.

He enjoys playing online games with his friends, loves the colors green and blue, and finds happiness in nature — especially going to the sea and camping in the mountains. He also loves Jollibee, and his favorite is their famous spaghetti.

And now, with a grateful heart and unwavering love, he is ready to spend forever with the woman he has loved for the past 10 years.

How We Meet
In 2015

Fate brought them back together when Jonah became their Dance Instructor during a playground demo at Talamban National High School. What started as a simple reunion and a casual "kamusta" unexpectedly reopened a connection between two hearts.

From that moment on, conversations became more frequent, laughter became more genuine, and their bond slowly grew deeper. Neither of them knew that the small moment of seeing each other again would become the start of a beautiful love story.

As days turned into months and years, their connection blossomed into a relationship built with love, patience, friendship, and dreams. And now, after all the memories they created together, they are finally preparing to begin their forever.
